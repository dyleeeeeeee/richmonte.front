# Open Graph Image Generation

To create the perfect OG image:

1. Use Figma/Canva/Photoshop
2. Dimensions: 1200 × 630 pixels
3. Design elements:
   - Background: Elegant gradient (gold to cream)
   - Logo: Your banner.png centered
   - Text: "Private Swiss Banking"
   - Subtitle: "Compagnie Financière Richemont SA"
   - Minimal, luxury aesthetic

4. Export as: opengraph-image.png
5. Place in: app/opengraph-image.png

Next.js will automatically use this for OG tags!

Quick template online: https://www.opengraph.xyz/
